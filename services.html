Primary Care Services

Our comprehensive primary care services focus on preventive medicine, early detection, and ongoing health management for patients of all ages.

General Medicine

🩺

Annual Health Checkups

Comprehensive annual physical examinations including vital signs assessment, medical history review, and preventive health screening.

  • Complete physical examination
  • Blood pressure and weight monitoring
  • Health risk assessment
  • Personalized health recommendations
🤒

Acute Illness Treatment

Prompt diagnosis and treatment of acute conditions including fever, infections, respiratory issues, and other immediate health concerns.

  • Fever management and treatment
  • Cold and flu treatment
  • Respiratory infection care
  • Same-day sick visits
💊

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing management of chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease with personalized treatment plans.

  • Diabetes management and monitoring
  • Blood pressure control
  • Medication management
  • Regular follow-up care

Preventive Care

💉

Vaccination Services

Complete immunization programs for children and adults, including routine vaccines, travel vaccines, and flu shots.

  • Childhood immunizations
  • Adult vaccines (flu, tetanus, etc.)
  • Travel vaccine consultations
  • Vaccine record management
📏

Growth & Development Tracking

Comprehensive monitoring of child growth and development milestones with early intervention when needed.

  • Height and weight tracking
  • Developmental milestone assessment
  • Nutritional guidance
  • Early intervention referrals
🔬

Health Screenings

Regular health screenings for early detection of diseases including cancer screening, cardiovascular risk assessment, and more.

  • Cancer screening programs
  • Cardiovascular risk assessment
  • Diabetes screening
  • Bone density testing

Diagnostic Services

🧪

Laboratory Testing

Comprehensive laboratory services including blood work, urinalysis, and specialized testing for accurate diagnosis.

  • Complete blood count (CBC)
  • Lipid panels and glucose testing
  • Urinalysis and cultures
  • Hormone level testing
📊

Imaging Services

Advanced imaging services including X-rays, ultrasounds, and EKGs for comprehensive diagnostic capabilities.

  • Digital X-ray imaging
  • Ultrasound examinations
  • EKG and stress testing
  • Bone density scans
🫀

Cardiac Testing

Comprehensive cardiac evaluation including EKGs, stress testing, and heart monitoring for cardiovascular health assessment.

  • 12-lead EKG testing
  • Stress testing
  • 24-hour heart monitoring
  • Cardiac risk assessment

Specialty Services

❤️

Cardiology
